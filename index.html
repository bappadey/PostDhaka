WHO WE ARE

Post Dhaka is a premier post-production boutique based in the heart of Dhaka, Bangladesh. We are a collective of passionate storytellers, editors, colorists, and visual artists dedicated to transforming raw footage into cinematic reality.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between technical excellence and artistic expression, we specialize in Commercials, Music Videos, Documentaries, and Fiction. Our workflow is designed to be collaborative, fast, and efficient.

At Post Dhaka, we don't just edit videos; we engineer emotions.
