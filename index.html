WHO WE ARE

Post Dhaka is a creative post-production and visual storytelling studio based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We specialize in professional video editing, color correction, color grading, motion graphics, and visual post-production services for films, web content, commercials, TVCs, OVCs, and digital media.

At Post Dhaka, we believe that every story deserves powerful presentation. Our team combines technical precision with creative vision to transform raw footage into cinematic, engaging, and emotionally impactful content. From short-form digital content to full-scale film projects, we maintain the highest standards of quality, consistency, and visual excellence.

We work with filmmakers, brands, agencies, and content creators to deliver polished, modern, and audience-focused visuals that stand out in today’s competitive media landscape. Our workflow is built on efficiency, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of storytelling and color science.

Whether it’s editing, color grading, or complete post-production finishing — Post Dhaka is your trusted creative partner for professional visual post-production.
